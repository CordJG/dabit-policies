# Dabit Privacy Policy

Jaekwan Lee ("Operator") complies with the Personal Information Protection Act and related laws to lawfully process personal information and manage it safely to protect the freedom and rights of data subjects.

This Privacy Policy applies to the use of the mobile application "Dabit" ("Service") provided by the Operator.

## 1. Personal Information Collected

### a. Required Information
- **Email Address**: Account registration, identity verification, password reset
- **Password**: Stored encrypted (Operator cannot view original)
- **Service Usage Records**: Habits, tokens, statistics data

### b. Optional Information
- **Nickname**: Display within the Service
- **Profile Image**: Display within the Service

### c. Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: OS type, OS version, device model
- **Error Logs**: Service stability improvement (Sentry)
- **Advertising Identifier**: For future ad service introduction

## 2. Purpose of Collection and Use

1. **Member Identification**: Registration, identity verification, fraud prevention
2. **Service Provision**: Habit management, reading logs, study time tracking
3. **Service Improvement**: Error tracking, anonymous usage pattern analysis
4. **Customer Support**: Inquiry responses
5. **Future Advertising**: With separate consent when ads are introduced

## 3. Retention Period

The Operator retains personal information while members use the service.

### a. Active Membership
- Retained for the duration of service use

### b. Upon Withdrawal
- All personal information and activity records are deleted immediately
- Deleted information cannot be recovered

### c. Legal Retention
When required by law for a specific period:
- Transaction records under e-commerce laws (if applicable)

## 4. Provision to Third Parties

The Operator does not provide personal information to third parties without the data subject's consent.

Exceptions:
- With separate consent from the data subject
- When required by law
- When provided in anonymized form for statistics

## 5. Outsourcing of Personal Information Processing

The Operator outsources personal information processing as follows:

| Outsourced To | Task | Retention |
|---|---|---|
| Supabase Inc. | Data storage and management (cloud) | Until withdrawal |
| Functional Software, Inc. (Sentry) | Error tracking and analysis | 90 days |
| Kakao Corp. | Book search API | Search term, temporary |
| Google LLC (AdMob) | Advertising service (when introduced) | Ad display period |

## 6. Data Subject Rights

Members may exercise the following rights:

1. **View Personal Information**: Available in the in-app settings page
2. **Modify Personal Information**: Direct modification in settings
3. **Delete Personal Information**: Through membership withdrawal
4. **Request Processing Suspension**: Via email

Rights can be exercised through the in-app settings page or email:
**dabit.help@gmail.com**

## 7. Technical and Administrative Measures

### a. Technical Measures
- Password encryption (bcrypt)
- HTTPS communication (TLS 1.2+)
- Database access privilege minimization
- Regular security audits

### b. Administrative Measures
- Minimization of personal information handlers (Operator only)
- Personal information protection education

## 8. Advertising Identifier

### a. Collected Advertising Identifiers
- Android Advertising ID (AAID)
- iOS Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA)

### b. Collection Time and Purpose
- **Current**: Not currently collected
- **Future Introduction**: Will be used for ad display and measurement with separate user consent

### c. Opt-Out Methods
- Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Reset/Delete advertising ID
- iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking > Allow Apps to Request to Track

## 9. Personal Information of Children Under 14

The Operator does not accept registration from children under 14.

If a child under 14 is found to have registered, membership is immediately revoked and all information is deleted.

## 10. Personal Information Protection Officer

The Operator is responsible for personal information processing and has designated a Personal Information Protection Officer:

- **Name**: Jaekwan Lee
- **Email**: dabit.help@gmail.com

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies from the effective date. When changes are made according to laws and policies, including additions, deletions, or corrections, notice will be given at least 7 days before the effective date.
