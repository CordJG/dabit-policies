# Dabit Account Deletion

This page exists so that you can request deletion of your Dabit account and associated data **even when you can't access the app**, in accordance with Google Play and Apple App Store policies.

- **App name**: Dabit
- **Developer**: Jaegwan Lee (Indie developer)
- **Contact**: dabit.help@gmail.com

---

## 1. Delete inside the app (recommended)

The fastest and safest way:

1. Open the Dabit app and sign in.
2. Tap **Settings** in the bottom tab bar.
3. Under the **Account** section, tap **Delete Account**.
4. Confirm the warning, then tap **Delete** again to confirm.

The deletion is processed immediately, and you will be signed out automatically.

---

## 2. Request deletion by email

If you can no longer install the app or sign in, you can request deletion by email.

- **Send to**: dabit.help@gmail.com
- **Subject**: `[Dabit Account Deletion Request]`
- **Include in the body**:
  - The email address you used to sign up
  - Your nickname (if you remember)
  - Reason for deletion (optional)

**Processing time**: Within **7 business days**. We will reply once the deletion is complete.

For verification, requests are only accepted from the email address used to register the account. Requests from other addresses may be ignored.

---

## 3. Data that will be deleted

The following data is **permanently deleted** when the account is removed.

- Account info (email, password hash, nickname, profile image)
- All habits and habit logs
- Stats, streaks, token balance
- Inventory: characters, backgrounds, accessories
- Reading data (bookshelf, progress, notes)
- Study data (timer logs)
- Device and push notification tokens

---

## 4. Data that will be retained

Some data must be retained for a limited period due to legal or operational requirements.

| Item | Retention | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| Payment receipt records | 5 years | E-commerce law (if applicable) |
| Error logs (Sentry) | Up to 90 days | Service stability |
| Anonymized analytics | Indefinite | Cannot be tied to an individual |

Original payment receipts are stored by Apple / Google billing systems. Dabit does not retain raw payment data.

---

## 5. Notes

- Account deletion is **irreversible**.
- You can sign up again with the same email after deletion, but previous data cannot be restored.
- If you have an active subscription, you must cancel it separately in your Apple / Google account. Deleting the Dabit account alone does not cancel an active subscription.
